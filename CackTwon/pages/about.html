Caketown,cake cafe, offers a large selection of different types of cake in the heart of Riyadh. Come for the sweet taste, stay for the coffee, and enjoy our extensive list of chocolate, vanilla cake, along with fresh coffee. With late night sitting area, take out, and delivery available until 1 AM, caketown will sure satisfy all your desserts cravings. The coffee bar offers different of latte, cappuccino and Arabic coffee.the cake kitchen churns out a wide range of specialty cake, for special occasions such as weddings, Birthdays, graduation parties,...etc. Caketown is located at 108 Alali Street in the Olaya neighborhood of Riyadh. Delivery is available within a 2.5-K radius for orders placed until 1 AM.
